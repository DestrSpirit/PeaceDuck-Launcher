Setting your RAM
Define the RAM allocated to your game, a larger amount will increase the performance of your game.
Note that you should avoid putting too much otherwise it can cause performance losses on your other applications.

-RAM Max: Indicates the total amount of RAM allocated to your game. It is recommended not to exceed 50% of the total amount of RAM on your PC.

-RAM Min: Indicates the minimum amount of RAM allocated when starting your game. It is recommended not to put more than 4GB for this value.
